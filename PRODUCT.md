# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Executives, managers, and operations staff at Journal, each with different needs from the same dashboard family:
- Executives: company-wide status at a glance (which channel is growing, which needs attention).
- Managers: channel-level diagnosis (Online/Offline/Consignment performance vs last year, drill into branches/platforms).
- Operations: near-real-time execution signals (today's numbers, alerts, what changed).

Job to be done: check business health fast, spot problems early, drill from summary to transaction detail without switching tools.

## Product Purpose

A no-framework PHP dashboard tracking sales performance across Online, Offline (retail), and Consignment channels for Journal. Every KPI shows the actual value plus its YoY growth (vs. the same period last year) so a viewer can tell at a glance whether a number is good or bad, not just what it is — there is no target/quota concept in this product; growth trend is the comparison baseline. Success = faster, more confident decisions than the previous reporting process, without needing a BI tool.

## Brand Personality

Professional, clean, fast. Reads as a precision instrument for people who need answers quickly, not a showcase.

## Anti-references

Explicitly NOT: colorful, chart-heavy, or decorative. No gratuitous charts that don't answer a specific business question. No dashboard-cliche gradient hero metrics or icon-heavy card grids. No color-only status signaling.

## Design Principles

- Actual value + YoY growth trend is the default framing for every KPI — never a bare number. (No target/quota framing: an earlier target/variance/achievement model was deliberately removed in favor of same-period-last-year comparison.)
- Summary-before-detail: Overview → Channel → Analysis → Transaction drill-down, never dump detail up front.
- Every chart must answer one specific business question — no decorative or "just in case" charts.
- Status indicators never rely on color alone — always pair with label, icon, or number.
- Consistency across channels: Online/Offline/Consignment dashboards should feel like one system, not three different tools.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No formal WCAG target (internal tool), but color-blind-safe status indicators are a hard requirement per Design Principles above — status must never rely on color alone.
